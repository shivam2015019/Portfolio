Frequency Comb

Studied the theoretical background of frequency combs, which are precise tools used for measuring and controlling the frequencies of light. These combs generate a spectrum of equally spaced frequencies, providing ultra-precise time and frequency measurements. Prepared a detailed report and delivered a presentation on the brief introduction to an optical frequency comb.

View on GitHub

Grover's Algorithm

Studied Grover's algorithm and successfully implemented it for 2 and 3 qubits using Qiskit and IBM Quantum Composer. This involved designing and executing quantum circuits to demonstrate the algorithm's efficiency in quantum search problems.

View on GitHub

Saturation Absorption Spectroscopy

Performed experiments in absorption spectroscopy and saturation absorption spectroscopy to investigate the number density of rubidium atoms in a rubidium vapor cell at various temperatures. Analyzed experimental data and performed curve fitting using the Voigt profile, a convolution of Gaussian and Lorentzian transmission curves, to represent the absorption spectra of the rubidium atom accurately.

View on GitHub
